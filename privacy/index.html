1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

In Short: We collect personal information you provide, information from connected accounts you authorize (such as a connected email inbox), information from your device and your use of the Services, and (if enabled) location and visit-related data.

A. Personal information you disclose to us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for an account, contact us, use the Services, or otherwise interact with us.

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Name (if provided)
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Contact or authentication data (such as account credentials or tokens used to authenticate you)

Notes on credentials: If you create an account using an email/password, we collect the password in a secure, hashed form. If you authenticate through a third-party sign-in method, we collect tokens or similar authentication data associated with that method.

B. Connected email inbox data (including content)

If you connect an email inbox through an authorization method (such as Google OAuth), we may access and process email data to provide the Services. This may include:

  • Email content (message body or portions of it, and snippets)
  • Email metadata (such as sender/recipient, subject line, timestamps, message identifiers, labels/folders, and header information)
  • Attachment content or attachment metadata, if and only if required to support a feature you use

Email content may include information about other people. Please avoid connecting accounts or processing emails that you do not have the right to use.

C. Location and visit-related data (including third-party SDK collection)

If you enable location permissions, we may collect and process location data to provide and improve the Services. Depending on your device and settings, this can include precise or approximate location.

We also use a third-party software development kit (“Location/Visit Measurement SDK”) to help collect and process signals that can support location/visit-related measurement and related service operations. Depending on your device settings and permissions, this SDK may collect and transmit the following types of data:

  • Device latitude/longitude and associated timestamps
  • Wi‑Fi scan data and/or Bluetooth/beacon scan data (as available on your device and permitted by your settings)
  • Unique identifiers such as an install identifier and/or an app-specific “consumer” identifier
  • Advertising identifier (such as IDFA/AAID) and your device’s advertising tracking preference/opt‑out setting
  • Basic device information (such as battery status, device model, and operating system/version)

This SDK may operate in the background when permitted by your device settings and permissions. We use the information from this SDK for purposes such as supporting location/visit-related measurement, improving reliability, preventing fraud/abuse, and operating and improving the Services. We do not use connected inbox data for advertising.

D. Information automatically collected (device, log, and usage data)

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate the Services. This information does not necessarily reveal your specific identity but may include device and usage information, such as:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers and app instance identifiers
  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Browser type (for the website)
  • Language preferences
  • Referring URLs
  • Dates/times of access
  • Diagnostic and performance data (including crash logs)

E. Push notifications

If you opt in, we may send push notifications related to your account or app functionality. You can turn these off in your device settings.

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

In Short: We process your information to provide and administer the Services, enable AI-assisted processing you request, support user feedback and troubleshooting, maintain security, and comply with law.

We may process personal information for purposes including:

  • Providing, operating, and maintaining the Services
  • Authenticating users and managing accounts
  • Processing connected inbox data and location/visit-related data to generate outputs within the Services
  • Associating user feedback with relevant inputs/outputs to improve reliability, troubleshoot issues, and support service quality
  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer support
  • Monitoring for security incidents, preventing fraud/abuse, and enforcing our policies
  • Analytics and performance monitoring (including debugging and crash analysis)
  • Complying with legal obligations and responding to lawful requests

We do not sell personal information. We do not use connected inbox data for advertising, including targeted advertising.

Depending on where you live and the context, we may rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

D. With your direction

We may share information when you direct us to do so or where you provide consent.

AI-assisted processing

We may use AI and machine-learning technologies to provide certain parts of the Services. To do this, we may send limited inputs (which may include portions of email content and/or location context) to third-party AI processing providers and receive outputs back.
